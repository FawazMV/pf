    About

    I'm a full-stack developer at Neutrinos — now an SDE-2, focused primarily on the front end in Angular. I build the user-facing side of enterprise automation products: in-house design systems, reusable component libraries, and visual drag-and-drop workflow builders — backed by hands-on Node.js / NestJS across the stack, for platforms used by Fortune 500 insurers.

    My biggest work is on Reels, Neutrinos' AI-powered process-automation platform: I led its UI revamp on Angular, PrimeNG and a JointJS canvas. On Alpha, I built a drag-and-drop form builder in Lit Elements that lets teams compose complex workflows with little to no code.

    On the backend I build scalable microservices in NestJS and Express on PostgreSQL — Dockerised and shipped through CI/CD — including a custom content service that replaced Strapi for Reels. I picked up the MER(A)N stack through an intensive bootcamp and freelance work, after a BSc in Physics.
